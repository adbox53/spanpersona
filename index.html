The Consistency-Seeker

Primary Persona – Span Longevity Movement Coach
“I want clear, gentle guidance — not more complexity.”

Risk Score

“How likely I am to overdo it today.”

Used to adjust intensity and reduce multi-day setbacks.

Goals Score

“How I’m doing this week toward consistent movement.”

Tracks sustainable weekly progress without streak pressure.

Goals

  • Build a sustainable weekly routine
  • Avoid soreness and setbacks
  • Maintain strength and mobility long-term
  • Fit movement into a busy schedule

Key Tasks

  • Complete 1-minute daily check-in
  • Review Risk & Goals scores
  • Select daily plan card
  • Occasionally complete squat screen

Behavior Patterns

  • “Weekend warrior” cycle
  • Prefers supportive guidance
  • Responds to clarity over metrics

Frustrations

  • Too many dashboards & metrics
  • Performance pressure
  • High cognitive load interfaces
  • Judgmental tone

Cognitive & UX Needs

  • Daily interaction ≤ 60 seconds
  • Clear explanation of Risk vs Goals
  • Low mental demand
  • Graceful fallback if camera fails

Success = 150–300 min moderate weekly activity, ≥2 strength sessions/week, fewer setback days, sustained engagement month-to-month.

The Restart-Focused Adult

“I want to exercise safely and consistently — not push hard and get set back again.”
Primary Motivation: Build a durable, sustainable movement routine that reduces injury risk and supports long-term health.

Goals

  • Exercise safely after time away
  • Reduce risk of reinjury
  • Maintain mobility and strength with age
  • Build consistency without pressure

Behaviors

  • Cautious about overexertion
  • Values clear, supportive guidance
  • Prefers stability over intensity

Frustrations

  • Performance comparisons & leaderboards
  • Pressure to increase intensity
  • Lack of guidance when restarting
  • Plans that assume durability

What Span Must Provide

  • Clear distinction between Risk and Goals scores
  • Actionable daily movement guidance
  • Preventative framing, not performance framing
  • Transparent recommendations
Success = steady weekly participation, strength & balance inclusion, reduced fear of reinjury, and sustained engagement over time.
